Medical Devices and In-Vitro Diagnostics Ontology (MDIVO): Description back to ToC

Ontology for the European catalogue of high risk medical devices and in-vitro diagnostic devices, aligned with EU Regulations MDR (2017/745) and IVDR (2017/746) and the EUDAMED database.

Rule_ImplantableActiveback to ToC or SWRL ToC

If a medical device has both isImplantable=true and isActive=true, it is classified as an ImplantableActiveDevice.

Medical Device c(?d) , is implantable dp(?d,"true"^^boolean) , is active dp(?d,"true"^^boolean) -> Implantable Active Device c(?d)

Rule_IVDFallsUnderIVDRback to ToC or SWRL ToC

If a medical device has isIVD=true, the reasoner infers that it falls under the IVDR regulatory framework.

Medical Device c(?d) , is IVD dp(?d,"true"^^boolean) -> Links a medical device to its applicable regulatory framework op(?d,?_IVDR_inst)

Rule_ManufacturerIsReportingEntityback to ToC or SWRL ToC

If an incident affects a device manufactured by M, then M is the reporting entity of the incident.

Incident c(?i) , affects device op(?i,?d) , is manufactured by op(?d,?m) -> has reporting entity op(?i,?m)
